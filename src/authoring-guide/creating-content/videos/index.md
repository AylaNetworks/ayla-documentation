Use an HTML <code>iframe</code> tag to add a video to your page:

<pre>
&lt;iframe 
  width="560" 
  height="315" 
  src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/aDdyFeo2A5E?rel=0&amp;showinfo=0" 
  frameborder="0" 
  allow="autoplay; 
  encrypted-media" 
  allowfullscreen&gt;
&lt;/iframe&gt;
</pre>

Here is the example in action:
